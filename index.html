About Azrael Group

Azrael Group is a design consultancy led by Tim McKenna.

Tim McKenna has spent the last 17 years working in design helping companies deliver results and find success. His experience spans a multitude of industries from finance, healthcare, government, education, and B2B product companies.He has led internal product teams at companies such as The Predictive Index, Procore Technologies, NIC Inc, and the American Academy of Family Physicians. In addition, he has led full service design capabilities for companies such as NIC Inc, Aspire Ventures, Own Up, and PHEAA.Tim has long been a contributor to the professional design community, through his mentorship program, podcasting and writing, speaking engagements, and volunteering with design organizations.
